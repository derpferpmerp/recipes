Simple Sloppy Joes


\[ \textbf{Ingredients} \]
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 Medium Onion, Chopped
  • ½ Cup Ketchup
  • 3 tbsp Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Water
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Dry Mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • Hamburger Buns

\[ \textbf{Instructions} \] Cook meat and onion in a large skillet until meat is browned; drain. Stir in next 7 ingredients, and head thoroughly. Serve on warm buns. Yield: 4 Servings.
