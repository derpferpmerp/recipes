Scalloped Potatoes

\[ \textbf{Information} \]
  • Prepares 4 cups in total

\[ \textbf{Ingredients} \]
  • 1 can Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 cups thinly sliced potatoes
  • ½ Cup thinly sliced onions
  • 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine

\[ \textbf{Instructions} \]
  1. Blend soup, milk, dash pepper.
  2. In buttered 1½-qt. casserole, arrange alternate layers of potatoes, onion, soup mixture, and cheese.
  3. Dot top with butter; sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Cover and bake at 375°F for 1 hour.
  5. Uncover; bake 15 min or more until done.
