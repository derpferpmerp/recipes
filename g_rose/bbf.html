Beer-Batter-Fried Mushrooms

\[ \textbf{Information} \]
  • Prepares 4-6 servings in total

\[ \textbf{Ingredients} \]
  • ½ Cup Flour
  • ¾ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ½ Cup Beer
  • 1 pound Fresh Mushrooms Salt

\[ \textbf{Instructions} \]
  1. Sift flour, salt, baking powder, and pepper into bowl.
  2. Blend in beten egg and beer; let batter stand 30 minutes. Meanwhile, clean mushrooms using damp towel, if possible, or quickly dip into water and pat dry.
  3. Cut into quarters, if large. Put a few mushrooms pieces at a time into batter, and stir with spatula to coat.
  4. Pick up and drain in slotted spoon or scoop. Fry in deep fat heated to about 375°F 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown. Turn once.
  5. Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with salt, and serve hot.
