Sweet-Sour Cucumbers

\[ \textbf{Information} \]
  • Prepares 3 cups in total
  • 10 minutes total preparation time

\[ \textbf{Ingredients} \]
  • ½ Cup each: water, distilled white vinegar
  • ¾ Cup Sugar -- Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Large Cucumber
  • ½ Large White Onion
  • 2 Jalapeno Peppers, thinly sliced

\[ \textbf{Instructions} \]
  1. Put water, vinegar, sugar and salt into small saucepan. Heat to boil; simmer until sugar dissolves. Remove from head, cool.
  2. Remove ends from cucumber (Peel if desired). Cut into ⅛-inch-thick slices. Cut slices into quarters. Cut onion into thin slices.
  3. Mix cucumber, onion and cooled vinegar mixture in large bowl. Mix well. Serve garnished with peppers.
